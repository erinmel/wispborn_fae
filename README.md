## About the Wispborn Fae

The **Wispborn Fae** are beings of pure, sentient magical energy condensed into stable humanoid forms. They are fundamentally **Fey** — magical, curious, and mysterious.

For complete lore, world history, and character creation options, see the [Wispborn Fae Race Guide](5e/EN/Wispborn_Fae.md).

## Acerca de los Wispborn Fae
Los **Wispborn Fae** son seres de pura energía mágica consciente condensada en formas humanoides estables. Son fundamentalmente **Fey** — mágicos, curiosos y misteriosos.

Para la historia completa, la historia del mundo y las opciones de creación de personajes, consulte la [Guía de Raza Wispborn Fae](5e/EN/Wispborn_Fae.md). *Próximamente en Español* (Vea el Roadmap)

## How to Use This Guide

### For Players
1. Read the [Wispborn Fae Race Guide](5e/EN/Wispborn_Fae.md) to understand race mechanics
2. Choose an elemental affinity and review that affinity's spell list
3. Use the character creation tables to flesh out your Wispborn's appearance and personality
4. Work with your DM to integrate your character into the campaign world

### For Dungeon Masters
1. Review the complete race mechanics and lore
2. Decide which elemental affinities fit your campaign world
3. Customize the Wispergrove and Fey court politics as needed
4. Use the spell lists as-is or modify them for your setting
5. Consult the roadmap for planned expansions and homebrew mechanics

### For Developers / Contributors
- All content is formatted in **Markdown** for easy editing in any text editor
- Structure follows D&D 5e convention (traits, spell lists, mechanics)
- Links are GitHub-compatible for seamless repository navigation
- Each spell affinity is a separate file for modularity and clarity
