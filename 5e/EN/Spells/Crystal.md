## Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Crystal Shard** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Conjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

Description:
You form a razor-edged sliver of crystal and hurl it at a target.

Mechanical Effect:
Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 piercing damage.
Damage increases to 2d8 at 5th level, 3d8 at 11th level, and 4d8 at 17th level.

---

**Prismatic Glint** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Illusion
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 round

Description:
You refract light through suspended crystal dust, dazzling a creature’s vision.

Mechanical Effect:
The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have disadvantage on its next attack roll before the end of
its next turn.

---

**Resonant Ping** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Divination
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V
Duration: Instantaneous

Description:
You emit a sharp crystalline tone that reveals hidden fractures.

Mechanical Effect:
You learn whether a creature or object within range has resistance, vulnerability, or immunity to thunder or force
damage (DM reveals yes/no only).

### 9th Level

[Index](#index)

**Prismatic Crystal Cataclysm** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th
School: Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 300 feet
Components: V, S, M (a flawless crystal worth at least 1,000 gp, which is not consumed)
Duration: Instantaneous

Description:
You call forth a towering eruption of prismatic crystal from the ground, forming jagged spires that refract devastating energy across the battlefield.

Mechanical Effect:
Choose a point you can see within range. Crystal spires erupt in a **40-foot-radius, 60-foot-high cylinder** centered on that point.

Creatures in the area must make a **Dexterity saving throw**.

• On a failed save, a creature takes **16d10 piercing damage** and **10d10 radiant damage**.
• On a successful save, the creature takes half damage.

The area becomes **difficult terrain** as massive crystal formations remain.

Additionally, until the end of your next turn, light refracting through the crystals creates blinding flashes. Creatures that start their turn in the area must succeed on a **Constitution saving throw** or be **blinded until the end of their turn**.

---

**Heart of the World Crystal** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th
School: Transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 minute

Description:
Your body becomes a perfect living crystal, channeling the ancient resonance of the earth’s deepest geodes.

Mechanical Effect:
For the duration, you gain the following benefits:

• You gain **100 temporary hit points**.
• You gain **resistance to all damage** except thunder.
• Your body emits brilliant refracted light in a **30-foot radius**.

Whenever a creature hits you with a melee attack, it takes **6d10 piercing damage** from explosive crystal shards.

Once per turn when you deal damage with a spell, you may cause crystalline energy to **chain to another creature within 30 feet**, dealing **6d10 radiant damage**.

When the spell ends, the crystal shell shatters violently. Creatures within **20 feet** must make a **Dexterity saving throw**, taking **8d10 piercing damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

**Infinite Geode Singularity** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th
School: Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 300 feet
Components: V, S, M (a flawless crystal worth 1,000 gp)
Duration: Instantaneous

Description:
You summon the catastrophic birth of a collapsing crystal star.

Mechanical Effect:
Choose a point within range. A collapsing geode of immense energy forms and detonates.

Creatures within a 50-foot radius must make a Dexterity saving throw.

On a failed save, a creature takes 18d10 force damage and 10d10 piercing damage.

On a successful save, the creature takes half damage.

The area becomes jagged crystal terrain, counting as difficult terrain for the next minute.
