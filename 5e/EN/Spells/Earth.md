## Spells

### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Stone Bolt** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

Description:
You hurl a shard of hardened stone.

Mechanical Effect:
Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 bludgeoning damage.
Damage increases to 2d6 at 5th level, 3d6 at 11th level, and 4d6 at 17th level.

---

**Mold Earth** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

Description:
You reshape earth and soil within a small area.

Mechanical Effect:
You can excavate or raise earth in a 5-foot cube (max 1 foot deep), create footprints, compact or loosen soil, or create
simple earthen structures. This cannot harm creatures or be used as an attack.

---

**Stone Sense** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Divination
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self (60-foot radius)
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Description:
You attune yourself to stone and earth vibrations.

Mechanical Effect:
You sense the presence and location of all stone, metal, and mineral deposits within 60 feet. You gain advantage on
Perception checks to detect tremors or movement through earth.

### 9th Level

Mythic mastery over earth, resilience, and the foundation of the world.

---

**Earth's Omniscience** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th
School: Divination
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 1 mile
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:
You become one with the earth, perceiving through stone and soil.

Mechanical Effect:
You sense all movement and vibrations within 1 mile through earth and stone. You can cast earth or stone-themed spells
as bonus actions and gain advantage on all spellcasting checks related to earth. You can speak telepathically with stone
elementals and earth creatures. You gain immunity to poison damage.

---

**Apocalyptic Upheaval** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th
School: Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 300 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:
You summon a world-ending geological cataclysm.

Mechanical Effect:
A 250-foot-radius sphere suffers apocalyptic upheaval. Every round:

- All creatures in the sphere take 16d12 bludgeoning damage (Strength save for half).
- Creatures that fail are knocked prone and buried 10 feet deep.
- All structures take 30d12 damage and collapse.
- Terrain becomes impassable chasms and stone formations.
- The sphere moves with you as a bonus action.

---

**Eternal Stone's Domain** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th
School: Transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:
You transcend mortal limitations, becoming an immortal being of stone.

Mechanical Effect:
You gain the following benefits for the duration:

- Unlimited burrowing speed through stone and earth.
- Immunity to all damage except adamantine and magical weapons.
- Can cast any Earth affinity spell as a bonus action without expending spell slots (once per spell level per turn).
- Immunity to poison damage.
- Can sense all creatures and vibrations within 1 mile through earth.
- Once per turn, when a creature you can see within 60 feet moves or acts, you can use your reaction to force it to make
  a Strength save or take 3d10 bludgeoning damage.
- You regain 30 hit points at the start of each of your turns while touching stone or earth.
