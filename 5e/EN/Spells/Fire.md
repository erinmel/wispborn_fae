## Spells


### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Flame Burst** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

Description:
You hurl a small ember that ignites in a brief flash.

Mechanical Effect:
Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 fire damage.
Damage increases to 2d6 at 5th level, 3d6 at 11th level, and 4d6 at 17th level.

---

**Ignite** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:
You set a small object or surface ablaze.

Mechanical Effect:
Target flammable object (not worn or carried) catches fire, shedding bright light in a 10-foot radius. A creature can
extinguish the flame with an action.

---

**Warmth Aura** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self (10-foot radius)
Components: V, S
Duration: 10 minutes

Description:
You radiate gentle, comforting heat.

Mechanical Effect:
You and willing creatures within 10 feet gain resistance to cold damage and advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks
made to resist freezing. The aura is visible as a faint red shimmer.

### 9th Level

[Index](#index)

**Flame's Omniscience** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th
School: Divination
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 1 mile
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:
You become one with all fires, perceiving through them.

Mechanical Effect:
You see through any non-magical fire within 1 mile. You can cast fire or heat-themed spells as bonus actions and gain

---

**Apocalyptic Cataclysm** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th
School: Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 300 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:
You summon a civilization-ending inferno that consumes all.

Mechanical Effect:
A 250-foot-radius sphere of apocalyptic flame forms. Every round:

- All creatures in the sphere take 16d12 fire damage (Dexterity save for half).
- Creatures that fail are knocked prone and pushed 30 feet.
- All structures take 30d12 damage.
- Terrain becomes difficult terrain and erupts with lava pools.
- All flammable material ignites instantly.
- The sphere moves with you as a bonus action.

---

**Eternal Phoenix's Ascension** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th
School: Transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:
You transcend mortal limitations, becoming an eternal infernal being.

Mechanical Effect:
You gain the following benefits for the duration:

- Unlimited flying speed (including hover).
- Immunity to all damage except cold water effects.
- Can cast any Fire affinity spell as a bonus action without expending spell slots (once per spell level per turn).
- Immunity to cold damage.
- Can ignite anything within 30 feet as a bonus action.
- Once per turn, when a creature you can see within 60 feet casts a spell or makes an attack, you can use your reaction
  to force it to make a Dexterity save or take 3d10 fire damage.
- You regain 20 hit points at the start of each of your turns.
