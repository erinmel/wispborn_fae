## Spells

### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Tidal Splash** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You hurl a concentrated surge of seawater at a creature.

Mechanical Effect:

Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 cold damage.
Damage increases to 2d8 at 5th level, 3d8 at 11th level, and 4d8 at 17th level.

---

**Salt Spray** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 round

Description:

A burst of briny spray stings the eyes.

Mechanical Effect:

The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have disadvantage on its next attack roll before the end of
its next turn.

---

**Current Step** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 bonus action

Range: Self

Components: V

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

A small wave propels you forward.

Mechanical Effect:

You move 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.

School: Divination

**Ebb and Flow** (Homebrew)

Level: 5th

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self (30-foot radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

You invoke the cosmic rhythm of the tides, creating alternating waves of protection and pressure.

Mechanical Effect:

The effect alternates each round. On odd rounds (1st, 3rd, 5th), allies within 30 feet gain +2 AC. On even rounds
(2nd, 4th), enemies within 30 feet have -2 AC. You choose which phase starts first when you cast the spell.
