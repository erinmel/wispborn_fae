## Spells

### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Spark Bolt** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You hurl a crackling bolt of electricity.

Mechanical Effect:

Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 lightning damage.
Damage increases to 2d6 at 5th level, 3d6 at 11th level, and 4d6 at 17th level.

---

**Arc Pulse** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You create a pulse of electricity that chains between nearby targets.

Mechanical Effect:

Make a ranged spell attack against one creature. On hit, you can target one other creature within 15 feet of the first
that you can see. If the original attack hit, make another ranged spell attack against this second creature. Each hit
deals 1d4 lightning damage.

---

**Static Aura** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self (10-foot radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

You surround yourself with crackling static electricity.

Mechanical Effect:

Creatures that start their turn within 10 feet of you or enter the area for the first time must make a Dexterity save or
take 1d4 lightning damage. You gain advantage on initiative rolls.

### 1st Level

[Index](#index)

**Chain Lightning Strike** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 60 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You fire lightning that chains between enemies.

Mechanical Effect:

Make a ranged spell attack against one creature within 60 feet. On hit, target takes 2d6 lightning damage and you can
target another creature within 15 feet of it. Make another attack against that creature. Chain continues for up to 3
targets.
Upcasting: +1d6 per slot above 1st.

---

**Electrokinetic Surge** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 bonus action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute

Description:

Electricity energizes your body, increasing your speed.

Mechanical Effect:

Your movement speed increases by 15 feet. You gain advantage on initiative rolls. Creatures within 5 feet of you have
disadvantage on their next melee attack against you.

---

**Conductive Barrier** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 reaction

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 round

Description:

Lightning shields you from incoming damage.

Mechanical Effect:

You gain 8 temporary hit points. A creature that hits you with a melee attack takes 1d4 lightning damage.

---

**Reactive Spark** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 reaction (when an ally within 30 feet is hit by an attack)

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You instinctively discharge a bolt of lightning at a creature that strikes your ally.

Mechanical Effect:

The attacker takes 2d6 lightning damage (Dexterity saving throw for half).

### 9th Level

[Index](#index)

**Lightning's Omniscience** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Divination

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 1 mile

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:

You become one with all electricity, perceiving through arcs and currents.

Mechanical Effect:

You sense all movement within 1 mile through electrical currents and static fields. You can cast lightning or
electricity-themed spells as bonus actions and gain advantage on all spellcasting checks related to lightning. You can
speak telepathically with any creature within 1 mile. You gain immunity to paralysis and lightning damage.

**Apocalyptic Maelstrom** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 300 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

You summon a civilization-ending electrical storm.

Mechanical Effect:

A 250-foot-radius sphere becomes apocalyptic electrical chaos. Every round:

- All creatures in the sphere take 16d12 lightning damage (Dexterity save for half).
- Creatures that fail are paralyzed and stunned.
- All structures take 30d12 damage.
- Terrain becomes arcing electricity fields.
- Metal objects melt and explode.
- The sphere moves with you as a bonus action.

**Eternal Storm's Domain** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:

You transcend mortal limitations, becoming an eternal being of pure electricity.

Mechanical Effect:

You gain the following benefits for the duration:

- Unlimited flying speed.
- Resistance to all damage.
- You can cast one Lightning affinity cantrip as a bonus action each turn.
- Immunity to paralysis and lightning damage.
- Can sense all creatures and electrical disturbances within 1 mile.
- You regain 10 hit points at the start of each of your turns.
