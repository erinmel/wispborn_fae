### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Umbral Spark** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip  

School: Necromancy  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: 60 feet  

Components: V, S  

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:  

You conjure a small mote of shadow and fling it toward a creature.

Mechanical Effect:  

Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit the target takes **1d6 necrotic damage**.

The shadow briefly clings to the creature, dimming light around it. The creature sheds **dim light in a 5-foot radius** until the start of your next turn.

Damage increases at higher levels:  
5th level: 2d6  
11th level: 3d6  
17th level: 4d6

---

**Shadow Whisper** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip  

School: Illusion  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: 60 feet  

Components: V, S  

Duration: 1 minute

Description:  

You speak through nearby shadows to communicate discreetly.

Mechanical Effect:  

You create a whispering voice originating from a shadow within range.

You can transmit **a short message of up to 25 words** to a creature that can hear the whisper.

A creature that succeeds on a **Wisdom (Perception) check against your spell save DC** realizes the voice is magical.

---

**Cloak of Dimness** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip  

School: Illusion  

Casting Time: 1 bonus action  

Range: Self  

Components: V, S  

Duration: 1 round

Description:  

You draw nearby shadows around your form, obscuring your position.

Mechanical Effect:  

Until the start of your next turn you gain **advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks**.

While standing in **dim light or darkness**, you also gain **half cover** against the first attack made against you.

### 1st Level

[Index](#index)

**Veil of Shade** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st  

School: Illusion  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: 30 feet  

Components: V, S  

Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Description:  

Shadows bend around a creature, disguising its presence from observers.

Mechanical Effect:  

Choose a willing creature. The creature gains **advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks**.

Additionally, creatures relying on **dark vision perceive the target as lightly obscured**.

At Higher Levels:  
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.

---

**Grasping Shade** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st  

School: Conjuration  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: 60 feet  

Components: V, S  

Duration: 1 minute

Description:  

Shadowy hands rise from the ground and cling to a creature's legs.

Mechanical Effect:  

The target must succeed on a **Strength saving throw**.

On a failure its **speed is reduced by 10 feet**.

The creature may repeat the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

At Higher Levels:  
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the speed reduction increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 1st (maximum 30 feet).

---

**Shadow Cache** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st  

School: Conjuration  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: Touch  

Components: V, S  

Duration: 8 hours

Description:  

You hide a small object within a fold of shadow, storing it in an extradimensional space.

Mechanical Effect:  

You touch an object that weighs **no more than 20 pounds** and store it in a pocket of shadow.

You may retrieve the object **as an action**.

If the duration ends, the object harmlessly appears at your feet.

At Higher Levels:  
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the weight limit increases by 10 pounds for each slot level above 1st.

**Umbral Step** (Homebrew)

Level: 2nd  

School: Conjuration  

Casting Time: 1 bonus action  

Range: 30 feet  

Components: V, S  

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:  

You step through shadows, teleporting between dark places.

Mechanical Effect:  

You teleport to an unoccupied space within **30 feet** that you can see which is in **dim light or darkness**.

School: Divination  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: Self  

Components: V, S  

Duration: 1 hour

Description:  

Your eyes adapt to the subtle flows of darkness, allowing perfect vision in shadows.

Mechanical Effect:  

You gain **dark vision out to 120 feet**.

If you already have dark vision, its range increases by **60 feet**.

You can also see through **nonmagical darkness normally**.

At Higher Levels:  
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the duration increases by 1 hour for each slot level above 2nd.

**Shade Double** (Homebrew)

Level: 2nd  

School: Illusion  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: 30 feet  

Components: V, S  

Duration: 1 minute

Description:  

You create a shifting shadow duplicate that mimics your movements.

Mechanical Effect:  

A duplicate appears in an unoccupied space within range.

Attack rolls against you have a **25% chance** to instead target the illusion.

The illusion disappears if hit by an attack or if you cast this spell again.

**Devouring Night** (Homebrew)

Level: 8th  

School: Necromancy  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: 120 feet  

Components: V, S, M (a void crystal worth at least 2,500 gp)  

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:  

You create a vortex of consuming shadow that devours creatures and drains their life force.

Mechanical Effect:  

Choose a point within range. A **30-foot radius** vortex of shadow appears.

Enemy creatures that start their turn in the area must make a **Constitution saving throw**.

### 9th Level

[Index](#index)

**Living Night** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th  

School: Conjuration  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: 120 feet  

Components: V, S, M (a fragment of the void worth at least 25,000 gp, which the spell consumes)  

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:  

You summon a colossal shadow entity from beyond reality, a creature of pure darkness.

Mechanical Effect:  

You summon a **Colossal Shadow Entity** in an unoccupied 20-foot by 20-foot space within range.

The entity has **AC 20**, **250 hit points**, resistance to all damage except radiant, and immunity to necrotic damage.

It makes three attacks per turn with **+15 to hit**, dealing **4d12+8 necrotic damage** per hit.

As a bonus action, you can command the entity to use one of these abilities:

- **Shadow Wave**: All enemy creatures within 30 feet take 6d10 necrotic damage (Dexterity save for half)
- **Darkness Aura**: Creates a 40-foot radius of magical darkness that moves with it
- **Life Drain**: One creature within 10 feet must make a Constitution save or have its hit point maximum reduced by 4d10

The entity obeys your mental commands and acts on your initiative.

**Endless Eclipse** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th  

School: Evocation  

Casting Time: 1 action  

Range: 1 mile  

Components: V, S, M (an eclipse diamond worth at least 50,000 gp)  

Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Description:  

You create an apocalyptic eclipse that plunges a massive area into eternal night.

Mechanical Effect:  

Choose a point within range. A **120-foot radius** of absolute darkness spreads from that point.

The area is completely dark, even to creatures with dark vision or true sight 
(except you).

Enemy creatures that start their turn in the area take **12d10 necrotic damage**.

Additionally, at the start of each of your turns, you can choose up to three creatures in the area to make a **Constitution saving throw**. On a failed save, a creature is **paralyzed** until the start of your next turn.

The darkness suppresses all light sources, magical or otherwise, except those created by 9th-level spells.

**Primordial Shade** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th  

School: Conjuration  

Casting Time: 1 minute  

Range: 300 feet  

Components: V, S, M (an artifact of pure shadow worth at least 100,000 gp)  

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:  

You call forth an ancient shadow titan from the dawn of darkness, a being of incomprehensible power.

Mechanical Effect:  

You summon a **Primordial Shadow Titan** in an unoccupied 30-foot by 30-foot space within range.

The titan has **AC 20**, **200 hit points**, immunity to necrotic and psychic damage, and resistance to all other damage types.

It makes three attacks per turn with **+15 to hit**, dealing **3d10+6 necrotic damage** per hit.

As a bonus action on each of your turns, you can command the titan to use one of these legendary abilities:

- **Umbral Apocalypse**: All enemy creatures within 60 feet take 6d8 necrotic damage (Constitution save for half)
- **Shadow Dominion**: Creates a 60-foot radius zone of magical darkness where enemies are frightened
- **Reality Tear**: Targets one creature, which must make a Charisma save or 
  be banished to the Shadow fell for 1 minute
- **Night's Grasp**: Up to 5 creatures within 120 feet must make Strength saves or be restrained and take 4d8 necrotic damage

The titan obeys your mental commands and acts on your initiative. When the spell ends, the titan leaves behind a permanent zone of shadow (20-foot radius) that remains as difficult terrain.
