## Spells

### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Frost Bolt** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You hurl a shard of jagged ice at a target.

Mechanical Effect:

Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 cold damage.
Damage increases to 2d6 at 5th level, 3d6 at 11th level, and 4d6 at 17th level.

---

**Chill Touch** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 10 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You touch a creature with numbing cold.

Mechanical Effect:

Target makes a Constitution save or takes 1d4 cold damage and has disadvantage on its next attack roll or ability check
before the end of its next turn.

---

**Frozen Aura** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self (10-foot radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

You surround yourself with a chilling aura of ice.

Mechanical Effect:

Creatures that start their turn within 10 feet of you or enter the area for the first time must make a Constitution save
or take 1d4 cold damage and be slowed (movement reduced by 10 feet until the end of their next turn).

### 1st Level

[Index](#index)

**Frost Strike** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 60 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You hurl a spike of ice that freezes and slows.

Mechanical Effect:

Make a ranged spell attack. On hit, target takes 2d6 cold damage and its movement speed is reduced by 10 feet until the
end of its next turn.
Upcasting: +1d6 per slot above 1st.

---

**Icy Grip** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 60 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

Ice forms around a creature, slowing it.

Mechanical Effect:

Target must make a Strength save or be restrained by ice. It can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns. The
restrained creature can break free with a DC 12 Strength check.

---

**Frigid Shelter** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 reaction

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 round

Description:

Ice shields you from harm.

Mechanical Effect:

You gain 5 temporary hit points. A creature that hits you with a melee attack takes 1d4 cold damage and must succeed a
Strength save or be slowed (movement reduced by 10 feet).

---

**Frosted Silence** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Illusion

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self (15-foot radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

You freeze the air around you into a stillness that swallows all sound.

Mechanical Effect:

A 15-foot-radius sphere centered on you becomes a zone of magical silence. No sound can be created within or pass
through the area. Creatures inside gain advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. Spells with verbal components cannot be
cast within the area.

**Ice Wall** (Homebrew)

Level: 2nd

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 60 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

You raise a wall of solid ice from the earth.

Mechanical Effect:

A 20-foot-long, 10-foot-high wall of ice emerges. It has AC 12 and 40 hit points. Creatures passing through take 2d6
cold damage. The wall is slippery (Dexterity checks have disadvantage to move across it).

---

**Preservation Field** (Homebrew)

Level: 2nd

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:

You preserve a creature or object with protective cold.

Mechanical Effect:

Target gains resistance to cold damage and doesn't age. Magical effects on the target are preserved without advancing (
spells don't count down or expire).

**Cryogenic Stasis** (Homebrew)

Level: 4th

School: Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: Up to 8 hours

Description:

You encase a willing creature in a cocoon of protective ice, preserving its life in frozen stillness.

Mechanical Effect:

The target becomes unconscious and is encased in ice. While encased, the target is immune to all damage, does not age,
and is stabilized if dying. The ice has AC 15 and 30 hit points. If the ice is destroyed, the spell ends. The caster can
end the spell early as an action.

### 9th Level

[Index](#index)

**Ice's Omniscience** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Divination

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 1 mile

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:

You become one with all ice, perceiving through frozen crystal.

Mechanical Effect:

You sense all movement within 1 mile through ice and snow. You can cast ice or cold-themed spells as bonus actions and
gain advantage on all spellcasting checks related to ice or preservation. You can speak telepathically with any creature
within 1 mile. You gain immunity to cold damage and aging.

---

**Apocalyptic Blizzard** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 300 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

You summon a civilization-ending blizzard of absolute cold.

Mechanical Effect:

A 250-foot-radius sphere becomes apocalyptic ice and blizzard. Every round:

- All creatures in the sphere take 16d12 cold damage (Constitution save for half).
- Creatures that fail are frozen solid and restrained.
- All structures take 30d12 damage and freeze.
- Terrain becomes impassable glacier and permafrost.
- All water freezes instantly.
- The sphere moves with you as a bonus action.

---

**Eternal Glacier's Domain** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:

You transcend mortal limitations, becoming an eternal being of ice and preservation.

Mechanical Effect:

You gain the following benefits for the duration:

- Unlimited movement across frozen surfaces and through ice.
- Resistance to all damage.
- You can cast one Ice affinity cantrip as a bonus action each turn.
- Immunity to cold damage and aging.
- Can sense all creatures and frozen things within 1 mile.
- You regain 10 hit points at the start of each of your turns while in a frozen area.
