## Spells

### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Water Dart** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You hurl a pressurized stream of water.

Mechanical Effect:

Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 bludgeoning damage.
Damage increases to 2d6 at 5th level, 3d6 at 11th level, and 4d6 at 17th level.

---

**Purify Water** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 10 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You cleanse water of impurities and toxins.

Mechanical Effect:

You purify up to 5 gallons of water, making it safe to drink. You can detect poison or disease in water as part of this
action.

---

**Tidal Sense** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Divination

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self (30-foot radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Description:

You attune yourself to the flow of water and moisture.

Mechanical Effect:

You sense the presence and location of all water sources within 30 feet (including groundwater, moisture in soil, and
blood in creatures). You gain advantage on Perception checks related to water or moisture.

### 1st Level

[Index](#index)

**Water Whip** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 60 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You lash a target with a whip of pressurized water.

Mechanical Effect:

Make a ranged spell attack. On hit, target takes 2d6 bludgeoning damage and is knocked prone.
Upcasting: +1d6 per slot above 1st.

---

**Fluid Form** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 bonus action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute

Description:

Your body becomes semi-liquid and malleable.

Mechanical Effect:

You gain advantage on Dexterity saving throws and can squeeze through spaces as small as 2 inches wide. Your movement
speed increases by 10 feet.

---

**Healing Tide** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 reaction

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 round

Description:

Soothing water surrounds you, mending wounds.

Mechanical Effect:

You regain 5 hit points. A creature that touches you or hits you with a melee attack gains advantage on its next saving
throw before the end of its next turn.

---

**Echoed Memory** (Homebrew)

Level: 1st

School: Divination

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You touch a creature and read the currents of memory stored in its body's water.

Mechanical Effect:

You experience the last 10 minutes of the target's sensory experience as a brief vision. A willing creature allows this
automatically. An unwilling creature makes a Wisdom saving throw to resist. This spell reveals what the creature saw,
heard, and felt, but not its thoughts.

**Leviathan Form** (Homebrew)

Level: 5th

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute

Description:

You transform into a creature of water and ocean fury.

Mechanical Effect:

You gain 80-foot swimming speed, resistance to fire and cold damage, and can move through water and around
corners without squeezing. Creatures that touch you take 2d6 cold damage.

---

**Whirlpool Trap** (Homebrew)

Level: 5th

School: Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 60 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Description:

You create a spinning vortex of water that traps enemies.

Mechanical Effect:

A 20-foot-radius cylinder of spinning water forms. Creatures starting their turn inside take 2d10 bludgeoning damage (
Strength save half). Creatures that fail cannot leave the vortex.

---

**Memory Tide** (Homebrew)

Level: 5th

School: Divination

Casting Time: 10 minutes

Range: Touch (a body of water)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You commune with a body of water, reading the memories stored in its currents.

Mechanical Effect:

You touch a natural body of water (pond, river, lake, or ocean). You may ask up to 3 questions about events that
occurred within 300 feet of the water during the past year. The water answers with brief sensory visions (sight and
sound only). The answers are accurate but may be incomplete.

### 9th Level

[Index](#index)

**Water's Omniscience** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Divination

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 1 mile

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:

You become one with all water, perceiving through it.

Mechanical Effect:

You see through any water source within 1 mile. You can cast water or flow-themed spells as bonus actions and gain
advantage on all spellcasting checks related to water or adaptation. You can speak telepathically with any aquatic
creature within 1 mile. You gain immunity to cold damage.

---

**Apocalyptic Deluge** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 300 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

You summon a civilization-ending flood that drowns all in its path.

Mechanical Effect:

A 250-foot-radius sphere of apocalyptic water and pressure forms. Every round:

- All creatures in the sphere take 16d12 bludgeoning damage (Strength save for half).
- Creatures that fail are knocked prone and pushed 40 feet.
- All structures take 30d12 damage.
- Terrain becomes deep water and flooding persists.
- All air pockets fill with water, suffocating non-aquatic creatures.
- The sphere moves with you as a bonus action.

---

**Eternal Leviathan's Domain** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:

You transcend mortal limitations, becoming an eternal creature of the depths.

Mechanical Effect:

You gain the following benefits for the duration:

- Unlimited swimming speed (including in any direction).
- Resistance to all damage.
- You can cast one Water affinity cantrip as a bonus action each turn.
- Immunity to cold damage.
- Can see perfectly in water and darkness.
- You regain 10 hit points at the start of each of your turns while in water.
