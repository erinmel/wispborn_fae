## Spells

### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Iron Spike** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Conjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

Description:
You conjure and launch a dense spike of iron.

Mechanical Effect:
Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 piercing damage.
Damage increases to 2d8 at 5th level, 3d8 at 11th level, and 4d8 at 17th level.

---

**Magnetic Flicker** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 round

Description:
You briefly distort the magnetic field around a creature.

Mechanical Effect:
If the target is wearing metal armor or wielding a metal weapon, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or have
disadvantage on its next attack roll before the end of its next turn.

---

**Resonant Strike** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 round

Description:
Your weapon vibrates with metallic resonance.

Mechanical Effect:
The next time you hit with a melee weapon attack before the end of your next turn, it deals an extra 1d6 thunder damage.

---
