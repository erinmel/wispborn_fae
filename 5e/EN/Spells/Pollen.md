## Spells

### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Irritant Spores** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Conjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 round

Description:
You release a puff of fine, shimmering pollen that irritates the senses.

Mechanical Effect:
The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 poison damage and have disadvantage on its next
attack roll before the end of its next turn.
Damage increases to 2d6 at 5th level, 3d6 at 11th level, and 4d6 at 17th level.

---

**Golden Drift** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 minute

Description:
You send a gentle cloud of pollen floating through the air.

Mechanical Effect:
A 5-foot cube becomes lightly obscured.
Small flying creatures inside the cube have their speed reduced by 10 feet.

---

**Verdant Mark** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip
School: Divination
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V
Duration: 1 round

Description:
You dust a creature in glowing pollen visible only to you.

Mechanical Effect:
You have advantage on your next attack roll against the target before the end of your next turn.
