## Spells

### Cantrips (Level 0)

[Index](#index)

**Rapid Slash** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You compress air into a razor-edged crescent and launch it forward.

Mechanical Effect:

Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 slashing damage.
Damage increases to 2d6 at 5th level, 3d6 at 11th level, and 4d6 at 17th level.

---

**Feather Touch** (Homebrew)

Level: Cantrip

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 10 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 round

Description:

You lighten a creature with a whisper of buoyant air.

Mechanical Effect:

The target ignores the first 10 feet of falling damage before the spell ends and gains advantage on its next Dexterity (Acrobatics) check.

---

**Gust** (Official Spell)

Level: Cantrip

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 30 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Description:

You briefly control the air in a small space.

Mechanical Effect:

You create one of the following effects:
• Push a Medium or smaller creature 5 feet (Strength save negates).
• Move a light object up to 10 feet.
• Create a harmless sensory wind effect.

### 9th Level

[Index](#index)

**Storm's Omniscience** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Divination

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 1 mile

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:

You become the storm itself, perceiving everything the wind touches.

Mechanical Effect:

You perceive all movement, sound, and vibrations within 1 mile as if you had blindsight. You can cast air or wind-themed spells as bonus actions and gain advantage on all spellcasting checks related to air, wind, or weather. You can speak telepathically with any creature within 1 mile.

---

**Cataclysmic Tempest** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 300 feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Description:

You summon a civilization-ending hurricane that tears the world asunder.

Mechanical Effect:

A 200-foot-radius sphere of apocalyptic wind forms centered on a point you can see. Every round:
- All creatures in the sphere take 15d12 slashing damage (Dexterity save for half).
- Creatures that fail are knocked prone and pushed 30 feet.
- All structures take 25d12 damage.
- Terrain becomes heavily obscured and difficult terrain.
- Unattended objects are obliterated.
- The sphere moves with you as a bonus action.

---

**Endless Zephyr's Domain** (Homebrew)

Level: 9th

School: Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Description:

You transcend mortal limitations, becoming a being of pure aerial essence.

Mechanical Effect:

You gain the following benefits for the duration:
- Unlimited fly speed (including hover).
- Resistance to all damage.
- You can cast one Air affinity cantrip as a bonus action each turn.
- Immunity to thunder and lightning damage.
- You can duplicate the effect of *Telekinesis* at will, moving objects or creatures up to 500 pounds with pure air pressure.
